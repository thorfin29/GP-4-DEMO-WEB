About us

Attraction in Sireh Park

The park’s name, “Sireh,” is derived from the Malay term for betel leaf, a traditional symbol of hospitality and respect in Malaysian culture. Reflecting this cultural significance, Sireh Park Johor is designed to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all who visit.

Conservation Area

Nature enthusiasts will appreciate the park’s well-preserved flora and fauna. Sireh Park Johor is home to a variety of plant species, including colorful flowers, towering trees, and vibrant shrubs. The park’s serene ambiance attracts a wide array of bird species, making it a paradise for birdwatching enthusiasts. Visitors can bring their binoculars and camera to capture the beauty of these feathered creatures in their natural habitat.

Fun Fact

At Sireh Park, you can book for camping or BBQ activities. You can also pay for Kayak activities here. You can also go for jungle trekking for free. Sireh Park will have thier own petting zoo in the near future. There’s a lot of small cafe near Sireh Park main entrance (they also sell cendol durian here!).
